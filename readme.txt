My Simple Tubes is a video widget which allows you to post your youtube videos on the sidebar of your blog.

== Description ==

In order to embed your youtube videos on the sidebar of your blog, all you need to do is to copy the video links on your youtube channel and paste them into the related widget input boxes. 

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Can I enter other video link besides youtube? =

No, because this widget can only use to display your youtube video.    

== Installation ==

1) Upload My Simple Tubes plugin to your blog, then activate it!
2) After that just use it likes how you use other widget.
